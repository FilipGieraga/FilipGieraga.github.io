Aimbot

Game is played over the period of 60 seconds, each dot is placed within it's grid container in a various randomly generated location. There is total of 30 rectangles where the dot can be placed and it can have a spot of top and left between (15% to 85%).

Hitting the dot gives you 10 points and missing it takes away 2 points.

Additional features:
Responsive Web Design
Local Storage Added
